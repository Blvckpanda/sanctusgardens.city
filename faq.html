Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about investing in Sanctus Gardens City. If you don't find what you're looking for, speak with our investment team.

Sanctus Gardens City is a 2,500-hectare masterplanned smart city on the waterfront of Ogbogoro, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Developed by Heri Homes and Properties Limited, it comprises five integrated districts — residential, commercial, leisure, innovation, and landmark — designed for 250,000 residents at full build-out. It is a purpose-built city, not a housing estate.
We offer a range of investment opportunities: freehold land plots (from 600 m²), Grade-A office leaseholds (from 500 m²), F&B operator pads (from 120 m²), lab suites, marina slips, and strategic partnerships. To access pricing and terms, unlock investment information on the Opportunities page or speak with our investment team.
