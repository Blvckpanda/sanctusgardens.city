Heri Homes & Properties

The development vision behind Sanctus Gardens City — a Nigerian real estate developer committed to purposeful, large-scale urban placemaking.

Company Overview

Purpose-built development

Heri Homes and Properties Limited is a Nigerian real estate development and urban investment company focused on large-scale, mixed-use, and masterplanned projects in the Niger Delta region.

With a mission to shape environments that elevate human potential, Heri Homes brings together expertise in infrastructure delivery, land development, corporate strategy, and sustainable urban design. The company is rooted in a deep understanding of the Niger Delta's unique urban, economic, and environmental dynamics.

Sanctus Gardens City represents the culmination of years of planning, stakeholder engagement, and design collaboration — a commitment to building not just structures, but a lasting legacy for Port Harcourt and Nigeria.

Developer since 2014
Mission

Our Purpose

To create masterplanned urban environments that generate long-term value for investors, residents, and the broader Niger Delta community — through responsible land stewardship, quality infrastructure, and visionary design.

Philosophy

Development Ethos

Every project begins with the land and the community. We prioritise infrastructure-first development, environmental sensitivity, and designs that respect the tropical climate and coastal ecology of the region.

Track Record

Proven Delivery

Heri Homes has successfully delivered residential and commercial land development projects in Rivers State. The company is backed by a management team with over 50 years of combined experience in real estate, finance, and infrastructure delivery.
